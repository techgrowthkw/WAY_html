Athoub al Twaim
CEO & Co-Founder

Athoub is a Marketing and business consultant qualified in 3 business majors, including an MSc in marketing branding and communications certified from the United Kingdom.

Athoub has pursued her business degrees and academic dissertations also from the United Kingdom.

Athoub is also a qualified business and marketing trainer that has launched online courses/seminars on GCC and global platforms.

Further in her career Athoub has continued to witness first-hand business owners struggles and challenges. Following a series of 7+ years in the business field Athoub decided to finally launch Way Marketing and Management Consultancy aiming in supporting businesses owners renovate their business strategy and make entrepreneurs believe that there is always a way towards solution.

The company strives to also achieve positive social impact by contributing to multiple humanitarian causes through its journey. Through her experience Athoub has not only solved severe cases but built long-term relationships with clients continuously supporting them through their journey.

Collaborating with business owners in overcoming obstacles and assisting them in leading a bright way.
